naming rules for variable 
-------------------------------


- always starts with alphabates 
            number1----valid
            1number----invalid


- white space is not allowed 
        number  1 -- invalid
        number_1---valid

- variable names are case-sensitive
        number1=10
        d.w(number1)-------invalid
        d.w(number1)-------valid

-never  use pre-defined keywords for variable name

        write, if , else , document.......
